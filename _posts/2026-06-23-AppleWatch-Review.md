# First Month Apple Watch Experince

About a month ago I bought an Apple Watch for the first time. I went to the Apple Store to look at the different watches and walked out with a series 11. For specifics I got the Jet Black GPS model with the blue sport loop band. 

I immediately started trying every workout feature I could, competing with friends who also had watches, and tracked my sleep to view my score and vitals. Overall, I was impressed with the amount of information I could get out of my apple watch. I really enjoy the AOD and all the watch faces that can match my style. One thing about the band is that it is fraying just within the month which I did not expect. I was told it wouldn’t and now wish I got silicone to start. 

One of the features that I enjoyed more than expected was Apple Fitness+. I thought I wouldn’t use the free trial and could end up canceling it before it was even over. However, the personal workout plans and trainer videos are way more motivating than I thought and help get me moving when I am not in the mood. 

I have done lots of runs, swim, polities, and biking and it’s nice to not have to rely on my phone anymore when doing these workouts. Even when I go on runs, I can download my music to my watch and leave my phone at home allowing me to be fully free while on my runs. 
Already without causing much damage my watch has some very small dents in it. It is more sensitive due to the jet-black color but it is disappointing because I thought I would last at least a year before I caused any noticeable nicks. 

A feature I thought I would use more is Apple Pay but I continue to just use that on my iPhone because you still must double tap on Apple Watch and that is harder to do than on iPhone for me.

Overall, my Apple Watch experience has been good. I have been wearing it everyday but as I get more used to wearing it, I take it off more when I realize I don’t really need it on every second of the day.
